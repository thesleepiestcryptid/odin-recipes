Cottage Pie

Description

This is a family favorite, usually made for Christmas. We double the recipe so that everybody ends up full and happy.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet dry French onion soup mix
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 1 (11 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced carrots, drained
  • 1 (10 ounce) can peas, drained
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.Reduce to medium-low
    and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare the mashed potatoes using your preferred method.
  4. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet
    until browned and crumbly, 5-7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Stir onion soup mix, chicken bouilllon,
    and beef bouillon into ground beef. Simmer until flavors blend, about two minutes. Add water if mixture
    is too dry.
  5. Spread ground beef mixture into the bottom of a 2 quart casserole dish. Top with corn, carrots
    and peas. Spoon mashed potato mixture over vegetable layer. Sprinkle with paprika.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over mashed potato layer. Continue
    baking until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 10 more minutes
